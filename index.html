Featured Case Studies

üì± E-commerce Reputation Analysis: Samsung vs Motorola

EDA + NLP Triangulation Crisis Detection Spanish Analysis 1,085 Reviews

Challenge: Extract actionable insights from limited dataset (1,085 reviews) scraped from MercadoLibre Argentina to identify reputation patterns and crisis indicators for smartphone brands.

Methodology: Automated web scraping (Playwright) combined with triangulation between quantitative analysis (ratings, engagement) and semantic clustering (TF-IDF, sentiment analysis) to validate findings through independent technique convergence.

Key Findings:

  • Automated Crisis Detection: Identified Samsung A15 charger issue 3+ months before rating impact
  • Root Cause Analysis: 99.1% of critical reviews concentrated on specific accessory problem
  • Competitive Differentiation: Motorola G32 showed superior consistency (3.95 vs 3.51 rating)
  • Methodological Validation: 80% convergence between EDA and NLP techniques

Business Impact: Framework enables proactive reputation management with early warning system, preventing minor issues from escalating to major crises.
